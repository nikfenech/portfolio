About Me

My name is Nik, I'm an Australian UX Designer. Originally from country Victoria, I'm now based out of Sydney - but always open to somewhere new and exciting.

Previously, I worked at Moroku, a fintech startup that aimed to gamify banking for from July 2016 to August 2017 and filled a number of roles working on products like Smart Saver, Chore Scout and other enterprise applications in a fast-paced environment.

I approach solving design problems like solving a puzzle, and make sure I immerse myself in research to understand the needs of the people I’m designing for.

While I'm primarily a UX Designer, I'm always learning new skills - I taught myself HTML, CSS and Javascript and used it to build this website among others while working at Moroku. Check out my github and codepen.

I’m currently finishing off the Freecodecamp front end certificate.
