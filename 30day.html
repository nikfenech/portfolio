30 Day Challenge

I’m a UX Designer first and foremost – but knowing HTML, CSS and Javascript means that I can make better prototypes in a shorter amount of time and communicate better with the people that work primarily in those formats.

To this end, I’m completing a small project each day to help consolidate these skills. Each will focus on a small aspect of front end skills and learning how to use them. Check the projects out below! See some wonky code or bad practice? Know a more elegant solution? I’d love to hear from you. Hit me up at nik.fenech@gmail.com.
